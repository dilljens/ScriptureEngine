# Frequency Layer

_Word occurrence patterns — distribution counts, hapax legomenon, repeated formulaic numbers (7, 10, 12, 40)_

> **Example**: The word 'covenant' (ברית) appears 13 times in Genesis, 25 times in Deuteronomy

## Purpose

The Frequency layer analyzes word occurrence patterns — how often words appear, where they cluster, hapax legomena (words appearing once), and numerical patterns in word counts.
