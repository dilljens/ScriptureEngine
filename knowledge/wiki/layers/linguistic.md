# Linguistic Layer

_Word-level language connections — same Hebrew/Greek lemma, root, morphology, wordplay, or cognate across passages_

> **Example**: Genesis 1:1 and John 1:1 share 'beginning' (בראשית / ἀρχῇ) — same semantic domain

## Purpose

The Linguistic layer tracks word-level connections between passages — same Hebrew roots, Greek lemmas, wordplay, cognates, and semantic domains. This is the most granular layer, operating at the word level rather than the concept level.
