# Numerical Layer

_Gematria and isopsephy connections — verses linked by matching numerical values of their words_

> **Example**: The gematria of 'Elohim' (אלהים = 86) connects to other verses where 86 appears

## Purpose

The Numerical layer connects verses through gematria (Hebrew) and isopsephy (Greek) — when words or phrases share numerical values, suggesting intentional numerical design.
