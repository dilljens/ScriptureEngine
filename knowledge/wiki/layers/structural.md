# Structural Layer

_Literary structures — chiasms, parallelisms, inclusios, refrains, acrostics, and formula markers_

> **Example**: Alma 36 forms a perfect chiastic structure: A-B-C-D-C'-B'-A'

## Purpose

The Structural layer identifies literary patterns within and between passages — chiasms, parallelisms, inclusios, refrains, acrostics, and formula markers like 'And it came to pass'.
