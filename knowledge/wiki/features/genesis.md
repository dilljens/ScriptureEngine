# Genesis

**Language**: Hebrew  **Verses**: 1,533  **Chapters**: 50

```
Primeval History (1–11)
├── Creation (1–2)
├── Fall (3–5)

└── Babel (10–11)
Patriarchal Narratives (12–50)
├── Abraham (12–25)

├── Jacob (28–36)

```

## Key Literary Patterns

### Toledot Formula
The word **תּוֹלְדוֹת** (toledot, "generations") appears 11 times as a structural marker:
1. Gen 2:4 — heavens and earth
2. Gen 5:1 — Adam
3. Gen 6:9 — Noah
4. Gen 10:1 — sons of Noah
5. Gen 11:10 — Shem
6. Gen 11:27 — Terah (Abraham)
7. Gen 25:12 — Ishmael
8. Gen 25:19 — Isaac
9. Gen 36:1 — Esau
10. Gen 36:9 — Esau (again)
11. Gen 37:2 — Jacob

### Seven-Day Creation Structure
Days 1–3 (forming) parallel Days 4–6 (filling):
- Day 1 (Light) → Day 4 (Luminaries)
- Day 2 (Waters/Heavens) → Day 5 (Fish/Birds)
- Day 3 (Land/Vegetation) → Day 6 (Animals/Humans)

- **28 letters** (7×4, also a triangular number: sum 1–7 = 28)
